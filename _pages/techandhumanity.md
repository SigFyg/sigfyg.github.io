How should technology best serve humanity? This is a question that deserves much reflection. I've had the opportunity to explore this question with many organizations, academics, and activists, most notably spending 2 years serving as president of Tech 4 Social Good at UMich (2022-2024). The culmination of my work, and my proposed approach to this question, is currently being organized into a journal publication (2024-2026, Advisor: Yousif Hasan). This page will be updated with the manuscript once it's complete!
